Before building the AI chat screen, build 
the Payment & HSA/FSA screen first. This 
is the most important screen in the app — 
it's what makes CareVoy different from 
Uber and Lyft. Design it with HSA/FSA as 
the primary payment method.

SCREEN LAYOUT:

Header: "Pay with Your HSA or FSA"
Subtext: "Your ride is a tax-free medical 
expense under IRS Code 213(d). Use your 
pre-tax dollars — automatically."

SECTION 1 — HSA/FSA Card (PRIMARY - show first)
- Large teal card at top labeled "HSA / FSA Card"
- Badge that says "TAX-FREE ✓" in green
- Input fields: Card number, Expiry, CVV
- Helper text: "Your HSA card works like 
  a debit card. Your FSA card is verified 
  automatically via our payment partner."
- Note: "An IRS-compliant receipt is 
  generated and emailed to you automatically 
  after every ride."

SECTION 2 — What's covered info box
- Teal outlined info box with icon
- Text: "Transportation to and from surgery 
  is 100% HSA/FSA eligible. CareVoy handles 
  the documentation so you never have to 
  file a reimbursement form manually."

SECTION 3 — Regular Card (SECONDARY)
- Smaller, muted section below
- Label: "Or pay with a regular card"
- Subtext: "We'll generate an HSA/FSA 
  reimbursement receipt you can submit 
  to your provider for refund."
- Card input fields same as above

SECTION 4 — Receipt & Reimbursement
- Toggle: "Auto-email receipt after 
  every ride" (default ON)
- Email field pre-filled from patient profile
- Text: "Your receipt includes everything 
  your HSA/FSA provider needs: date, 
  provider, amount, and IRS expense code."

SAVE BUTTON: "Save Payment Method"
- Saves card token to patients table
  (hsa_fsa_card_token and/or stripe fields)
- Shows confirmation: "HSA/FSA card saved. 
  You're ready to ride."

After saving, when a ride is confirmed 
automatically show: "Your HSA/FSA card 
will be charged $[amount] after your ride 
completes. Receipt sent to [email]."

Wire this screen to:
1. The Review step (Step 3) in Book a Ride 
   flow — if no card saved, prompt to add one
2. A "Payment & Receipts" tab on home screen

Same dark navy styling. HSA/FSA section 
should feel premium and trustworthy — 
this is the moment patients realize 
CareVoy saves them real money.
