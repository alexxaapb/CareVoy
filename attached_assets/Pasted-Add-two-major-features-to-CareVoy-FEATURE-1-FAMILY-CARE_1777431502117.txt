Add two major features to CareVoy:

FEATURE 1: FAMILY CAREGIVER MODE

Add support for caregivers booking 
rides on behalf of patients. Adult 
children booking for elderly parents 
in assisted living is the actual 
use case for our biggest market.

Database changes:
Create caregivers table:
- id (uuid, primary key)
- created_at (timestamptz)
- caregiver_user_id (uuid, references auth.users)
- patient_id (uuid, references patients)
- relationship (text)
- consent_signed_at (timestamptz)
- consent_method (text)
- can_book_rides (boolean default true)
- can_view_receipts (boolean default true)
- active (boolean default true)

Onboarding flow update:
After patient creates account, ask:
"Are you booking for yourself or 
someone in your care?"
- Myself
- Someone in my care
- Both

If "Someone in my care" or "Both":
Show caregiver setup:
- Person's full name
- Their phone (optional)
- Their date of birth
- Their address
- Relationship
- Digital consent: "I have permission 
  to book rides and manage receipts 
  for [name]"

Home screen update:
Show context switcher at top:
"Booking for: [Yourself ▾]"
Tap to switch between people in care

When viewing as caregiver for 
patient X:
- Booking flow uses their address
- Their HSA card is used (or 
  caregiver's card with note)
- Rides show in their history
- Receipts go to caregiver email 
  AND patient email

Caregiver dashboard:
Shows all people in care:
"Mom — Next ride Thursday 9am"
"Dad — No rides scheduled"

FEATURE 2: CALENDAR SYNC

Add ability to connect Apple Calendar 
or Google Calendar to detect medical 
appointments and proactively suggest 
ride bookings.

Settings → Calendar Integration:
- Connect Apple Calendar (iOS)
- Connect Google Calendar
- OAuth permissions

When connected, app scans calendar 
for events with keywords:
"appointment", "doctor", "surgery", 
"dialysis", "therapy", "checkup",
"hospital", any pre-loaded facility name

When match found, push notification:
"You have an appointment Thursday 
at 9am at OhioHealth Riverside. 
Want to book a CareVoy ride?"

Tap notification → opens booking 
flow with date, time, and location 
pre-filled.

Privacy: Only read calendar events, 
don't write anything. Don't store 
calendar data — just check during 
sessions.

Both features should respect role 
permissions and HIPAA. Caregiver 
mode requires patient consent stored 
in caregivers table. Calendar sync 
requires explicit OAuth permission 
that user can revoke.
