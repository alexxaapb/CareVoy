Add Family Caregiver Mode to CareVoy.

NEW DATABASE STRUCTURE:
Create caregivers table:
- id (uuid, primary key)
- created_at (timestamptz)
- caregiver_user_id (uuid, references auth.users)
- patient_id (uuid, references patients)
- relationship (text) — 'spouse', 'child', 
  'parent', 'grandchild', 'guardian', 'other'
- consent_signed_at (timestamptz)
- consent_method (text) — 'verbal', 'written', 'digital'
- can_book_rides (boolean, default true)
- can_view_receipts (boolean, default true)
- can_view_medical_info (boolean, default false)
- active (boolean, default true)

ONBOARDING UPDATES:
After patient creates account, 
add a screen:
"Are you booking rides for yourself 
 or someone else?"
- Myself
- Someone in my care
- Both

If "Someone in my care" or "Both":
  Show caregiver setup flow:
  - Enter person's full name
  - Their phone number (optional)
  - Their date of birth
  - Their address
  - Your relationship to them
  - Confirm: "I have permission to 
    book rides and manage receipts 
    for [name]" → digital consent

CAREGIVER HOME SCREEN:
Show toggle/switcher at top:
"Booking for: [Yourself] | [Mom] | 
 [Add another]"

Selecting different person changes 
context throughout app:
- Booking flow uses their address
- Their HSA card is used
- Ride history shows their rides
- Receipts go to caregiver email 
  AND patient email if available

CAREGIVER DASHBOARD:
Show all people in their care 
on a single dashboard:
- Mom: Next ride Thursday 9am
- Dad: No rides scheduled
- Aunt Linda: Ride completed yesterday

Tap any person to switch context.

PERMISSIONS:
Patient (if they use the app) sees:
"Sarah Daughter is your caregiver"
in their settings. Can revoke 
permission anytime.

If patient doesn't use app at all:
Caregiver is full admin of their 
profile.

DATABASE UPDATES NEEDED:
- patients table: 
  Add managed_by_caregiver (boolean)
- rides table:
  Add booked_by_user_id (uuid) — 
  may differ from patient_id
- payments table:
  Add paid_by_user_id (uuid) — 
  may differ from patient_id
- notifications table:
  Add notify_caregiver (boolean) — 
  if true, send to caregiver too

This is the assisted living killer 
feature. Adult children booking 
for elderly parents is the actual 
use case.
